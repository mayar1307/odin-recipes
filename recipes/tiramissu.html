Tiramisse

Description

A soft amazingly-tasting, chocolaty, sweet, and yummy snack

Ingredients

  • Espresso
  • Cheese
  • Egg yolks
  • Granulated sugar
  • Heavy Cream>
  • Ladyfinger Cookies
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Make coffee and let it cool
  2. Mix egg yolks and sugar until creamy
  3. Add mascarpone and mix until smooth
  4. Whip cream until stiff peaks form
  5. Gently fold whipped cream into mascarpone mix
  6. Dip ladyfingers quickly in coffee
  7. Place a layer of dipped ladyfingers in dish
  8. Spread half of cream over cookies
  9. Add another layer of dipped ladyfingers
  10. Spread rest of cream on top
  11. Dust with cocoa powder
  12. chill in fridge for atleast 4 hours
