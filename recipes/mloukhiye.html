Mloukhiyye

Description

This dish is hearty, flavorful, and perfect for family meals — especially on weekends or special occasions. It's typically served with a side of toasted pita bread and lemon wedges.

Ingredients

  • 500g frozen or fresh mloukhieh leaves (jute leaves)
  • 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces) or 500g beef chunks
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Cooked white rice (for serving)
  • Toasted pita bread (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken or beef in water with the onion, salt, and pepper until cooked. Remove meat and set aside; strain and keep the broth.
  2. In a pot, heat oil or ghee, then sauté the minced garlic and coriander until fragrant.
  3. Add the mloukhieh leaves and stir well until they slightly darken and shrink.
  4. Pour in the reserved broth and bring everything to a gentle boil.
  5. Add the cooked chicken or beef to the pot and simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  6. Finish with freshly squeezed lemon juice and adjust seasoning to taste.
  7. Serve hot over a bed of white rice with toasted pita bread and lemon wedges on the side.
